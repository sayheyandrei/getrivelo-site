Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Rivelo respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you sign in, create lists, and share them with other people.

Information we collect

Account information: email address and sign-in identifiers provided by Apple, Google, or email one-time codes (OTP).

Profile information: display name and optional avatar photo if you choose to upload one.

App data: shopping lists, list items, and sharing/membership information (who a list is shared with and their access role).

How we use information

We use this information to authenticate you, keep your lists in sync across devices, enable sharing with people you invite, and operate and improve the app.

Where data is processed

Rivelo uses Supabase (database, authentication, storage, and realtime services) to provide account features such as sign-in, syncing, and sharing. Apple and Google may process sign-in information as part of their authentication services.

Sharing

When you share a list, people you invite can access the shared content according to the permissions you grant. Do not share sensitive information in lists.

Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. We may retain limited information if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Contact

For questions or data requests, contact us at sayheyandrei@gmail.com.
