Rivelo Support

Need help with sign-in, sharing, purchases, or account deletion? Here are the fastest ways to reach us and solve the most common issues.

Common topics

  • Sign in: Apple, Google, and email one-time codes.
  • Didn’t receive the email code: Check Spam or Junk, wait 1 to 2 minutes, then tap Resend.
  • Sharing: Invite people to a list; shared lists stay in sync.
  • Subscriptions: Manage or cancel in iOS Settings, Apple ID, then Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases: Open Rivelo, then Settings, Premium, and Restore Purchases.
  • Account or data deletion: Email us from the address used in Rivelo with subject "Delete my account".
