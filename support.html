Common topics

  • Sign in: Apple, Google, and email one-time codes.
  • Didn't receive the email code: Check Spam/Junk, wait 1–2 minutes, tap Resend (available after 60 seconds).
  • Sharing: Invite people to a list; shared lists stay in sync.
  • Subscriptions: Manage/cancel in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases: Rivelo → Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.
  • Account/data deletion: Email us from the address used in Rivelo with subject "Delete my account".
